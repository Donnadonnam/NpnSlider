What is Multi Range slider?

It is a range slider with two handler. You can select a range of values at a time.

Example1

SelectedRange: [{{currentValues[0]}} - {{currentValues[1]}}]

Step: 1

Example2

SelectedRange: [{{currentValues2[0]}} - {{currentValues2[1]}}]

Step: {{step2}}
